ABOUT EPSILON 2022

Epsilon is an Academic Convention wherein the invited Industry Experts will speak about their expertise in the field of Internet Spectrum.This time, we have got covered security over various domains like websites, 5G and IOT, everything from Blockchains to cybersecurity and many more. Our target is to get around this vast domain starting right from the genesis and pan it up to the conceptual level.


TRACKS FOR EPSILON

IEEE Track

"Blockchain" is an unchangeable distributed digital record of transactions or data stored in multiple places on a computer network, with many use cases beyond cryptocurrencies. We'll see how advanced security is achieved via the cybersecurity frameworks, and secure coding practices by getting hands-on on smart contracts.

Computer Society Track

Cyber Security refers to protecting cyber space including critical information infrastructure from attack, damage, misuse and economic espionage.Under CS Track of Epsilon 2022, we have got you covered all about Cybersecurity and its different aspects.

MTT-S Track

5G and IoT Security. IoT is here to transform our lives and 5G will enable it. It'll make daily lives effortless and highly dependent on technology. But with greater easy there are also greater risks with Security. In this track we'll be covering the elementary of it while envisioning a safe future in IoT.

FAQs

What is Epsilon 2022?

Epsilon is a three day academic convention organized under IEEE SIESGST from 3nd to the 15th of August, 2022.

Is there any registration fee?

It is completely free for everyone. All it takes is your interest and time

Why should I participate?

Epsilon provides a great opportunity to learn the selected topics all the way from the beginner to expert level. Participants will receive certificates and best performers will receive prizes!

Who can participate for Epsilon 2022?

Anyone who is inclined towards learning new emerging technologies.
