ABOUT EPSILON 2022

Epsilon is an Academic Convention wherein the invited Industry Experts will speak about their expertise in the field of Internet Spectrum.This time, we have got covered security over various domains like websites, 5G and IOT, everything from Blockchains to cybersecurity and many more. Our target is to get around this vast domain starting right from the genesis and pan it up to the conceptual level.

TRACKS FOR EPSILON

Subtopic For IEEE

"Blockchain" is an unchangeable distributed digital record of transactions or data stored in multiple places on a computer network, with many use cases beyond cryptocurrencies.
In this track, we'll be understanding how advanced security is achieved via the cybersecurity frameworks, security testing methodologies, and secure coding practices while understanding by getting hands-on on smart contracts.

Subtopic For CS

Cyber Security refers to protecting cyber space including critical information infrastructure from attack, damage, misuse and economic espionage.Under CS Track of Epsilon 2022, we have got you covered all about Cybersecurity and its different aspects.

Subtopic For MTTS

We're connected to the internet through a various devices like our phones, smart watches, etc.Our lives are significantly simplified by these gadgets. But have you ever considered how much it would simplify things for us if we connected more and more gadgets to the internet? The Internet of Things is a new technical development, and it has here. The issue of security and safety, however, also arises. The Internet of Things (IoT) will be more interconnected thanks to the super-fast 5G network, which will also enable more efficient data transfer. Let's dive into the world of 5G and IoT
